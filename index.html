WHO'S THAT POKEMON?!

Welcome to Who's That Pokemon! You will be guessing the name of a randomly chosen Pokemon. The list is LONG - 807 Pokemon have officially been released so far! Do you have what it takes to be the very best?

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start" button to play
  2. Guess a single letter by typing it in the prompt
  3. Repeat until all lives are lost or you finish the Pokemon Name
  4. Play again!
